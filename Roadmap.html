The EVFY Sustainability Roadmap

Our Sustainability Index is a program that provides green conscious member the platform to make informed carbon reduction decisions, with levels of services that consumer are consumers are comfortable with

As part of the program, EVFY offers cosumers subscription tiers and a roadmap to make the most informed decision. This includes monthly report, access to green products and services and offers a forum for members to share green lifestyle tips and communtity to help other to become more 'green'.

EVFY Provides Sustainable Lifestyle Choices.

The Sustainability Index assigns a green score to everyday products and services so you can have a consolidated sustainability score. Now you can have an easy way to see how your day-to-day decisions impact the world around you. You can also have fun sharing your score with friends and family too!

Choose your Sustainability Tier.

Simply select the sustainability tier that works best for you and your 'green' lifestyle. The more sustainable you are, the higher your sustainability index will become. This ensures taht you will also receive the highest discounts and rewards from our favorite sustainable partners.

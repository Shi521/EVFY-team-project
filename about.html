About EVFY

EVFY – a cutting-edge clean tech start-up – offers green solutions, starting with electric mobility, to help communities live more sustainable lives. With EVFY, consumers become members of our sustainability program, known as The Sustainability Index. This program provides substantial savings and rewards for being a conscious consumer aiming to live carbon free. Through our program, EVFY aims to help people become as carbon-free as possible. We educate and guide on lifestyle changes and provide services and resources to 'go green'. The EVFY Sustainability Index is the new benchmark for consumers to understand the environmental impact of their decisions and social capital to share amongst peers for a broader goal of making a world-wide impact.
